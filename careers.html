How we work

Small team. Long horizons. Story first, always.

Studio Concentric is built around a small group of people who care deeply about the craft of producing. We move slowly when slowness matters and quickly when speed matters. We protect the artists we work with.

We're remote-flexible with hubs in Los Angeles and New York. We hire for taste, judgment, and follow-through.
